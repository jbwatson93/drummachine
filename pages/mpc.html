MPC Clone

The MPC is a digital sampling drum machine and MIDI sequencer designed by Roger Linn and produced by Akai from 1988 onwards. The MPC had a major influence on the development of electronic and hip hop music. The MPC is a full on sampler and sequencer and midi production center. Anything you can think of creating is possible with this machine.
